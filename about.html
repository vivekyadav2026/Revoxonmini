Our Story

Building Trust Through Uncompromised Quality

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the piping industry, Revoxon Industries Pvt. Ltd. has emerged as a trusted name in manufacturing premium PVC, UPVC, CPVC, and SWR pipes across India.

Our journey began with a simple commitment: to provide durable, reliable, and innovative piping solutions that cater to the evolving needs of agriculture, plumbing, and industrial sectors. Today, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and rigorous quality control processes ensure that every Revoxon pipe stands the test of time.
